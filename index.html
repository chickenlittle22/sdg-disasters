Welcome

Welcome to our website about natural disasters and SDG 1.5. This site is designed to help people learn about different types of natural disasters, how they form, and how to stay safe. We also created a game that connects to this topic so you can learn in a more interactive way. Our goal is to spread awareness and show why being prepared can save lives.

Why This Matters

Natural disasters affect millions of people every year around the world. They can destroy homes, schools, and entire communities in just minutes. Many people are not prepared or do not understand the warning signs. By learning about disasters and how to respond, we can reduce injuries, deaths, and damage. Awareness is one of the most important steps toward staying safe.

What You Will Learn

  • How natural disasters form
  • How to prepare for emergencies
  • How climate change affects disasters
  • How communities can stay safe

Featured Topics

On this website, you will learn about different types of natural disasters such as earthquakes, hurricanes, tornadoes, floods, wildfires, and tsunamis. You will also learn how these disasters form, the warning signs to look for, and important safety tips. We also explain how this connects to SDG 1.5 and why disaster preparedness is important for communities worldwide.
